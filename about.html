Our Story

A Legacy Rooted in Excellence

Founded in 1990, A&R Group began as a small family-driven venture in Kolkata with a singular vision: to build homes that outlast generations. What started with a handful of residential projects has grown into one of India's most trusted real estate brands, spanning six cities and thousands of happy families.

Our founders believed that a home is more than four walls — it's a promise. A promise of quality, transparency, and timeless craftsmanship. That belief remains at the core of everything we do, from the materials we source to the communities we create.

Today, with over 150 completed projects and partnerships with leading architects and planners, A&R Group stands as a symbol of trust, innovation, and enduring value in Indian real estate.
